Serra Console's First Live Stream

The members of Group 9 Serra Console launched their very first gaming livestream on
Facebook last February 3, 2023, at 2:00 PM The streamers of the group,
Sean and Ellis, played Doors on Roblox,
which is a first-person horror game with the goal of reaching
the last door at Level 100, while avoiding entities that will attempt
to kill the players within the lobby. The members aside the streamers on the other hand,
naming Rhian, Raven and Aleah took part in technical and moderator duties.
